About Me

I'm a native San Franciscan and graduated from college in 2019, spending my college days working as a bartender while paying off my student loans. I applied to law school with an 165 LSAT score and worked as an assistant paralegal post-grad. I switched paths when the pandemic began, teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at home. My years experience studying legal logic made for a seamless transition to attacking computer science logic. I found a new passion in this and decided to enroll in App Academy to learn full-stack development. My goal is to find a home at a company which values diversity and can help challenge me as an engineer to build technology that will make people's lives easier. Outside of coding, I live an active lifestyle. You can find me running Lake Merced, at 24 hour fitness, hiking, golfing, snowboarding, playing in basketball leagues, or practicing martial arts. Growing up in SF made me a socially inclusive and extroverted person — I love connecting with new people!
