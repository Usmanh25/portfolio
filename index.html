About Me

My name is Usman. I am a San Francisco native and graduated from college in 2019. Holding a B.A. in philosophy, I spent my early adolescence taking courses in logical reasoning and critical thinking. I applied to law school with an LSAT score of 165, and began coding when the COVID pandemic began, teaching myself HTML, CSS, & Javascript online. Before the pandemic, I worked at Fragomen LLP as an assistant paralegal. Prior to that I worked as a bartender in college while paying off my student loans. My passion in computer technnology was seemless, as I found many similarities between the thought processes of decoding logic in computer code and decoding logic in a logic game. I decided in November 2021 to attend App Academy coding Bootcamp to expedite my learning process in full-stack development.
